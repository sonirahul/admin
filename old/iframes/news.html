Spain Visa Application Centre opens in Tangier - Morocco
The Spain Visa application center officially opens in Tangier, Morocco on the 27th Sep 2012 by the Consul General of Spain to Tangier; Mr. Arturo Reig Tapia .

Spain Visa Application Centre opens in Tetuan - Morocco
Spain Visa application center officially opens in Tetuan, Morocco on 26th Sep 2012 by the Consul General of Spain to Tetuan ;Mr. Carlos Diaz Valcarcel .

Spain Visa Application Centre opens in Amman - Jordan
The Spain Visa Application Centre in Amman opened its door to the public on the 08th of Jan 2012, this the first outsourced Schengen VAC in the entire Kingdom of Jordan. .
