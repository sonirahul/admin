Corporate

Provides business management services through our full service corporate travel division. The Department operates teams that are dedicated to managing, maintaining, and servicing your specific account. In addition, we analyze your travel patterns, provide monthly management reports, and make recommendations for additional cost-saving programs. We look forward to the opportunity to set up your corporate travel account! Our Corporate travel unit currently services reputed multinational companies and key government organizations that would vouch for the high standard of service.

We provide following services to our corporate clients:
» Round the clock service. (24/7)
» Dedicated staff to handle each account
» Dedicated email & mobile facility
» Free travel documents delivery service in the Kuwait
» Kuwait Airport Business class lounge access. (optional)
» Hala meet & assist service. (optional)
» Airport transfers. (optional)
» Attractive airline fares
» Negotiate specific airline deals
» Attractive holiday packages
» Implant service. (optional)
» Credit facilities

We make the following commitment to all our Customers:

Quality Service
All correspondence will receive a response within 48 hours (by email or fax). In most cases, you can expect to receive a response within 24 hours. We attempt to meet your requirements 99% of the time.

High Level of Customer Servicing
All our clients have access to our 24-hour Customer Service Hotline when travelling worldwide. If they have any difficulties or requirements, they can contact our staff on the telephone for immediate attention.

Complaint Handling
While we Endeavour to have a perfect success rate with all our customers, problems will sometimes arise. In the event that they do, we undertake to fully investigate all aspects of the problem within a 2-days period.

Customer Service Unit
All our clients will have access to our designated team on 24-hour a day, 7 days a week basis. They are trained to handle and resolve any complaint on the spot.

Account Relationship Management
We are geared towards achieving customer service and satisfaction. Our practice of multi-level relationship with our clients start from the Sales & Marketing to the Reservations & Customer Service Unit right down to the accounts Department. All our customers have access to the person who is responsible for their account
