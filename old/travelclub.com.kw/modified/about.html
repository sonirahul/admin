About Us

Our Mission

“We are committed to become a benchmark in travel management company in Kuwait through excellence innovation and strategic partnerships”.

Travel Club is owned by Mawared House - Kuwait, fully licensed and BSP accredited travel Management Company offering a broad range of travel products. Travel club exploded onto the travel scene in January 2007 and is focusing to grow as one of the largest, most successful and most respected travel companies in Kuwait by employing number of highly experienced, multi-lingual travel consultants to provide the best possible service for our valuable clients. Where teams of experienced and knowledgeable staffs provide high level of support required to meet the needs of the ever-expanding number of clients.

Your Travel with friends and family is your most precious time together and no-one understands that better than us! Our team of professionals negotiates all prices and each property is inspected to ensure that it conforms to strict health and safety standards. Working very closely with our colleagues our worldwide suppliers, we are constantly seeking to exceed our customers' expectations, both in terms of the value for money and quality of the service we provide.

We also provide dedicated travel related service through our retail and corporate travel departments. Corporate Travel Direct offers our client base a 24-hour service that quickly understands individuals and your corporate needs. Corporate Travel Direct successfully delivers cost effective travel solutions to many of Kuwait’s leading business houses. Apart from the many other benefits, our customers enjoy a 24-hour support service through our fully functional call center and visa services through our VFS service center. More and more corporate customers are choosing Travel Club as their Travel Planner Company in Kuwait .

The company image is firmly based on an ethos of value, quality, choice and performance in every aspect of the business. It has taken years for Travel Club to establish this highly regarded reputation. Thus, we intend to protect and nurture this reputation and enhance our image as a modern, progressive organization delivering a unique global service to the market. As an independent organization, the company is able to offer clients the benefits of a vast choice of products and services, from both global chains and independent suppliers. The company cultivates a culture of innovation and excels at introducing new concepts to the market. These are the key elements that drive our market leading position. We provide our partners with the best business arsenal in existence. Travel Club is built on best prices, exemplary personal attention, professional commitment, long term partnerships with clients, and quality service. Through our commitment to excellence and staff performance, and our positive, focused and responsive attitude and usage of the most up-to-date information and technology available, we will offer the highest standards in service, value and quality to all our customers irrespective of race, gender or religion.
